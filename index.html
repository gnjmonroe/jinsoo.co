ABOUT

I'm a freelance UI/UX designer and web developer based in Seoul, primarily working on architecture-related projects.


I'm currently interested in how graphic and interface formats that we take for granted (e.g., maps, spreadsheets, search engines) can be taken from their conventional contexts and applied (in the DOM) elsewhere to create novel experiences. I'm also interested in how notions of mess can be thoughtfully incorporated into the web.


I've studied Urban Design, Urban Planning, and Materials Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan. Previously, I worked as a product engineer in the auto industry, shepherding the 2018 Ford Edge center console, in its 118+ permutations, to production.
