ABOUT

I'm a freelance front-end developer based in Seoul. Sometimes I design the projects I develop.


I started out by making exhibition and portfolio websites for architects, usually applying motifs and aesthetics from architecture and urban design to navigational design (e.g., subway maps and korean advertisement stickers). These days I focus more on building and maintaing information systems and digital tools. I still do design/develop projects occasionally.


Previously, I worked as an in-house graphic designer and web developer at a local architecture firm. Before that, I worked as a product engineer in the auto industry, shepherding the 2018 Ford Edge center console, in its 118+ permutations, to production. I have degrees in Urban Design , Urban Planning , and Materials Science and Engineering from the University of Michigan.
